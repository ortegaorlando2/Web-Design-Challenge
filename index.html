Latitude can be precisely determine with current technology. However, Latitude is not the only factor determining the perfect place to spend vacation. Many other factors contribute to the weather at a given place. In this project, we use real data from weather stations in cities of the world to visualize and compare the influence of latitude on four different elements that a traveler would be interested in when planning a vacation. The idea is to help the traveler understand what are the odds of predicting weather conditions based exclusively on latitude information.

Important Note:

It is also important to understand that the rotation of the Earth around the sun and the tilt of the Earth's axis are fundamental aspects of weather prediction. This characteristic of the Earth is responsible for the seasons observed in Mid-to-High latitudes which in turns interact with the weather in the tropics to modify local variations in climatic conditions.


According the BBC's Bitesize website, there are four important factors that affect climate at a given time

  1. Latitude or distance from the equator
  2. Prevailing wind
  3. Altitude or height above sea level
  4. Distance from the sea
